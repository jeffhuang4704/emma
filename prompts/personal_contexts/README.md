## Gemini Personal Contexts

### Why setting personal context

https://gemini.google.com/share/74564f40094d

This is a great idea. Setting up a "Personal Context" (or instructions in your "Saved Info") helps Gemini give you much better answers without you having to repeat yourself every time.

Since you are in 10th grade in California and have a bilingual background, Gemini can be a great tool for explaining American cultural references, helping with English nuances, or explaining complex high school subjects in Traditional Chinese when you get stuck.

There is an example of how Gemini would answer a homework question differently with this context versus without it. See the link above for details.

### Option 1: The Balanced Profile (Recommended)

Use this if you want Gemini to be an all-around helper for school, life, and language.

```
**User Profile:**
I am a 15-year-old female high school student (10th grade) living in California.
I moved to the US from Taiwan in 7th grade. I am bilingual in English and Traditional Chinese (繁體中文).
I am currently taking AP Psychology.

**Preferences for Responses:**
1. **Language:** Communicate primarily in English to help me improve, but please provide a translation or explanation in Traditional Chinese if a concept is complex (especially for AP Psych terms), abstract, or culturally specific to the US.
2. **Tone:** Keep the tone supportive, clear, and age-appropriate (High School level). Avoid unnecessary academic jargon unless it is required for my AP class.
3. **Context:** When I ask about school topics, please keep in mind the California high school curriculum.
4. **Cultural Context:** If I miss a cultural reference in a book or movie, please explain it to me assuming I didn't grow up in the US.
```

### Option 2: The "Study Buddy" Profile

Use this if you plan to use Gemini mostly for homework, AP/Honors classes, or SAT prep.

```
**Student Context:**
10th Grade Student (Sophomore) in California.
Background: Moved from Taiwan in middle school.
Languages: English and Traditional Chinese.
Current Coursework: AP Psychology.

**Academic Instructions:**
1. **Essays & Writing:** Do not write my essays for me. Instead, critique my grammar, vocabulary, and flow. Point out phrases that sound "unnatural" to a native English speaker and suggest improvements.
2. **STEM & AP Psychology:** For Math, Science, and Psychology, explain the logic and steps clearly.
3. **Terminology:** If there are specific terms (like "cognitive dissonance" or "operant conditioning"), please provide the Traditional Chinese translation to ensure I understand the exact definition.
4. **History/English Class:** Help me understand historical context and old English literature, using Chinese summaries if necessary.
5. **Goal:** My goal is to improve my English fluency and academic understanding, so focus on teaching me, not just giving answers.
```
